During the spring of 2020 ByteHacks aims to host a Bay Area hackathon open to middle and high school, promoting them to build applicable projects and learn fundamental programming knowledge

Frequently Asked Questions

What does one do at a hackathon?

A hackathon is an overnight event in which participants work in groups in order to develop and program their own project.

How much coding experience do I need?

Absolutely none! BayHacks happily welcomes beginner programers and utilizes a series of workshops in order to develop skills.

What am I supposed to build?

Participants are allowed to build anything they can imagine within the time period. Different projects may be more applicable to certain rewards.

What should I bring?

A valid school ID, signed waivers, a laptop, charger(s), and toiletries. Remember to bring your hardware as well if you would like to use it.

Who can attend OmniHacks?

All highschool and middleschool students are allowed to attend with no regards to previous coding experience.

How much does it cost to attend?

BayHacks is 100% free for participants thanks to our wonderful sponsors!
