World Connect aims to help high school students in Fuhsd connect with their culture and ethnic background. Diverse students are often forced to join uniform classes such as Spanish and French. Many ethnic students are therefore unable to further their understanding of their mother tongue

Frequently Asked Questions

What is World Connect?

World Connect is our plan that hopes to introduce new ethnic language classes offered in high school in order to create an inclusive environment for all students.

Why is World Connect necessary?

World Connect is necessary since many Indian, Southeast Asian, South American, etc. students are unable to further their understanding of their mother tongue since the class is not offered at school.

How will World Connect be achieved?

With the help of people like you, we hope to gather at least 500 signatures and then take our plan to the FUHSD Board of Trustees.

What is the Board of Trustees?

The Board of Trustess is a small group of people who control all major decisions that affects schools, student, and teachers in the district. The Board is blind to the issue that World Connect is trying to fix.

Why can't we leave the current system in place?

This allows students to get increased practice and utilize what they learned in the real world, ultimately offering them an extra incentive to learn, instead of just for grades and graduation requirements, which should not be the target for taking a class..

What happens without World Connect?

How does World Connect work?

World Connect would be centered around the major ethnic groups who are not currently represented in the world language department.

How applicable is World Connect?

With the rapid expansion and growth of various cultures throughout the US, the applicability of adding new languages would be enormous.

How do I join the program?

If you want to join our program its a simple and easy process and all you need are these two things: Engagement with the course material and Positive support for the new language program

How easy is the implementation process?

Implementing new language classes will be a simple transition as pre-existing classrooms can be used as the location of the new program and other rooms used for pre-existing language courses can be reused.

Who will teach the program?

Locating qualified teachers will not be an issue due to the fact that these new diverse languages will be spoken by a massive amount of people within the region.

What is the overall purpose?

Many students may want to learn about and connect with their own language and culture in order to communicate with their loved ones and get a better sense of their background.
